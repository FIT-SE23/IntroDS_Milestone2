\begin{IEEEbiography}[{\includegraphics[width=1in,height=1.25in,clip,keepaspectratio]{people/TEDx_Leontidis.jpg}}]{Dr. Georgios Leontidis}
is the Director of the Interdisciplinary Centre for Data and AI, and a Reader in Machine Learning at the University of Aberdeen, UK, leading the Machine Learning group. Before joining the University of Aberdeen, Georgios was a Senior Lecturer at the University of Lincoln, UK. His expertise lies within the areas of Capsule Neural Networks, Domain Adaptation, and Self-Supervised Learning. Georgios has applied his research in different settings, including agri-food, gas turbines, nuclear reactors, environment, retail refrigeration systems, and healthcare. Georgios has been leading several projects, funded by industry, such as Siemens Energy and Tesco, EU-H2020, and UKRI. He is also a TEDx speaker and has featured in public media.
